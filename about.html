Why I started this site..

(contact me below)

This website came about from my background as a professional golfer. I was a player in my younger days and progressed to teaching golf full-time for about 30 years.

I took an interest in trading about ten years ago and learned everything there is to know about the industry. My financial situation did not allow me to trade for real money, but I kept up my trading knowledge and learned more during the past few years.

From my extensive research over the years, one thing stood out very clearly. Most of the trading education offerings online are from retail traders and marketers. None of them are qualified professionals! I hold professionals in their respective industries in high esteem and don't have any regard for a retail trader calling himself a "professional," 

Professionals have qualifications and are usually members of a governing body. Right?
So my question is: Why pay for and receive training from an "amateur."

The resources on professional investing are all qualified professionals in trading and investing. Only the best for you!

Enjoy and have fun,
Louis
